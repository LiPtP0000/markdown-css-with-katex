## Signal and Continuous Signal

- signal
  - continuous signal
    - continus time signal：时间连续、幅度不一定
    - Analog signal：时间连续、幅度连续
  - discrete signal
    - digital signal：时间幅度均不连续
    - discrete time signal: 时间离散、幅度连续
